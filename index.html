About Us

We are the coding community at NIT Sikkim. We help students sharpen their programming skills, tackle real-world problems, and grow as developers through hands-on learning and friendly competition.

Resource Distribution

We curate and share learning resources for coding, web development, app development, and more to help you level up your skills.

Doubt Sessions

Got stuck on a concept? We run regular hands-on sessions where members learn together and clear doubts through practical problem-solving.

Coding Competitions

We host regular coding contests and hackathons to challenge your skills, build your portfolio, and prepare you for national-level competitions.
